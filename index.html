About Me

Damilola Onitiju

Product Designer


I design clean, appealing, and functional interfaces which comply with the latest web accessibility standards. My design process is user-centered and I know what brands need to capture their audiences' attention.

With a Bachelors degree in Computer Science, I am skilled at carrying out social, cognitive and data-driven research to understand the context of use and the user experience.

I have great understanding of front-end development,software testing and product testing. I am proficient in the use of design, wire framing and protyping tools like Figma, AdobeXD, Adobe Photoshop, Draw.io and Zeplin.
